## 📱 About the app

Tempo is a minimalist launcher for your Android phone. It helps you focus by removing distractions from your home screen. The app includes a strict blocker. This tool locks selected apps for ten days at a time. The system survives app reinstallation. Users cannot bypass this limit once enabled. This feature encourages better digital habits by removing the urge to check social media or games during your workday. The app architecture uses Kotlin and Jetpack Compose to ensure smooth performance on all devices.

## 🛠️ Setting up your home screen

Android phones require you to select a specific app to act as the home screen. Follow these steps to make Tempo your primary launcher:

1. Open your phone settings app.
2. Search for the menu labeled Default Apps.
3. Tap Home App.
4. Choose Tempo from the list of available launchers.
5. Press your home button. Your screen should now look clean and simple.

You can return to these settings at any time if you wish to use a different launcher.

## 🔒 Using the app blocker

The core feature of Tempo is the ten-day app blocker. This tool helps you build self-control. Use it for apps that cause stress or waste your time. 

1. Open the Tempo settings menu from your home screen.
2. Find the App Blocker section.
3. Select the apps you want to lock.
4. Confirm your choice. 
5. The selected apps will disappear from your launcher for exactly ten days. 

The lock persists even if you try to delete or reinstall the blocked applications. This serves as a forced break from digital clutter. Ensure you only lock apps you truly want to avoid.

## ✨ Common questions

**Does this app track my data?**
Tempo does not collect your personal information. All your settings stay on your phone.

**What happens after the ten-day lock ends?**
The apps you locked will return to your list. You can choose to lock them again for another ten days if you still feel they act as a distraction.

**Can I uninstall Tempo?**
Yes. You can uninstall the launcher like any other app. However, if you have active locks, the restrictions remain in place until the ten-day period concludes, even if you switch back to your old launcher.

**How do I update the application?**
Check the releases page occasionally for new versions. Download and install the new file over the old one to receive the latest improvements.
